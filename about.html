Our Story

From a small angithi to a name people trust

Since 2008

Desi Angithi Dhaba started with one simple dream – serve authentic North Indian dhaba food with the same taste, aroma, and warmth found on Indian highways.

Our recipes are passed down through generations, using freshly ground masalas, pure ghee, and traditional cooking methods.

  • 🔥 Live Tandoor Cooking
  • 🌿 Fresh & Hygienic Ingredients
  • 🍲 Slow-Cooked Family Recipes
  • 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family & Group Friendly

Our Quality Promise

We follow strict hygiene standards, source fresh vegetables daily, and prepare food only after your order is placed. No shortcuts. No compromise.
